Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Description:

This customizable and nuritious smoothie recipe is just right for those hot, summer days and New Years resolutions. Feel free to substitute and add to this recipe as you like!

Try this recipe out and please let us know your thoughts in the comment section below!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps:

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!
